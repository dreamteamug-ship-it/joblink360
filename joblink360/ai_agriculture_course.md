### Module 1: Introduction to AI in Agriculture  
**Learning Objectives:**  
By the end of this module, you will understand the basics of how artificial intelligence is being used in agriculture and its impact on farming practices.

*Core Content:*  
- Overview of AI in agriculture (e.g., crop prediction, disease detection, supply chain optimization).  
- African examples:  
  - Crop disease detection and diagnosis.  
  - Weather prediction for climate resilience.  
  - Soil health monitoring and optimization.  

*Key Takeaways:*  
- Artificial intelligence is revolutionizing agricultural practices.  
- Farmers use AI tools to monitor crop health, predict weather patterns, and optimize resources.  

**Practical Exercise:**  
Create a simple AI tool that predicts the yield of your local crop based on historical weather data from your region. You can use machine learning algorithms or existing agricultural models available online.

*Quiz Questions:*  
1. What is an example of AI being used in agriculture?  
   A) Cloud-based software for crop prediction  
   B) Farmers using GPS devices to track soil health  
   C) Farmers relying solely on visual inspection of plants  
   D) Farmers using traditional hand-harvesting techniques  

2. Which factor is most critical for accurate weather prediction in African agriculture?  
   A) Temperature  
   B) Precipitation  
   C) Soil type  
   D) Rainfall patterns  

3. What does "soil health" refer to in agricultural terms?  
   A) The amount of organic matter in soil  
   B) The structure and composition of soil  
   C) The presence of bacteria or fungi that decompose organic matter  
   D) The use of soil for farming purposes
